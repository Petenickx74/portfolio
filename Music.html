Music

I make music.

I joined a band in my softmore year of highschool as a singer. It wasn't long before I branched out, teaching myself to play guitar, how to sequence, and how to play the drums. I began to record with my computer and started putting songs together.

Throw Me Down a Well

Throw Me Down a Well is a rock and roll band that started in Portland, Maine. Justin Norton, Booth Hemingway, and myself joined forces and began jamming out some interesting material. Dylan Collins later joined as our bass player. The music is at times heavy, moody, and chaotic. Here's a link to the official website: Throw Me Down a Well

American Royål

In 2008 I started a project under the name American Royål. By 2013 I released my first offical album. One review that never happened said "This album is fun, at times hard hitting, but has a little sum'thing for everyone." You can hear it in the soundcloud player above, the tracks have this picture.
