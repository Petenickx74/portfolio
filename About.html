About

I started drawing at a young age, maybe 4 or 5. I was captivated with using my imagination, making up characters and creatures and having them spring to life across the page. Like all kids I was a sponge, and a friend of mine, Ben and I were into super heros. We would draw super heros at school during class and become them at reccess. We knew them all and what powers they possesed. And so I became familiar with anatomy, learning where muscles weaved together and what they looked like as the body moved.
