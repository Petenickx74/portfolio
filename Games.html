Games

Growing up in what I consider a golden age of gaming, I became influenced by companies like Sierra, Lucas Arts, Westwood Studios and Blizzard. I'm a big fan of pixel art, and in my exploration of the potential of game making started building sprite sheets and using Unity to grasp the basics of game making.

RPG in Unity 5 + PyxelEdit & Photoshop

After doing some research and watching other people create the kind of 2d art I enjoy, I came across a program called PyxelEdit. I found PyxelEdit to be much like Photoshop, but it's catered for pixel art specifically. I use it in conjunction with Photoshop for sprite sheets and background art.
