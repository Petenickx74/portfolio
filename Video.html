Deserted Island

A Flash animation I made in college in 2012. Its mostly an intro and first few establishing shots, but I had fun working on it. The premise is as follows: Monkey Island meets Metalocalypse, the mega popular band Throw Me Down a Well's plane goes down while flying over the Caribbean on the way to a show. The guitar player/singer finds himself washed up on shore of a deserted island, or so he thinks..


Moto

This one was also done in 2012. It was animated in a rotoscope style. Premise: A motorcycle chase scene envisioned for a story about a modern Vampire Hunter/Thief named Peter Lok.
